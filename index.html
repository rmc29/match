Instructions
This is an implementation of the card game Set.
Select groups of three cards.

Cards have four properties:
  • shape (square, oval or zigzag),
  • colour (red, green or blue),
  • shading (empty, shaded or full), and
  • number of shapes (1, 2 or 3).
You score three points if you select a group of cards which, for each property, are either all the same or all different.

You lose one point if you select a group of cards which do not fulfil this.

Equivalently:
There must be no property which two of your cards share and the third does not.

When you select a correct group, the cards are removed and replaced.
